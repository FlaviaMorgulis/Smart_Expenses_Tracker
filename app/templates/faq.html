Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SmartExpensesTracker

How do I add a new transaction?

Navigate to the Transactions page and click the "Add Transaction" button. Fill in the transaction details including amount, category, and description, then click "Save".

Can I edit or delete existing transactions?

Yes! In the Transactions page, you'll find edit and delete buttons for each transaction. Click the edit button to modify transaction details or the delete button to remove it.

How are my spending categories organized?

SmartExpensesTracker uses predefined categories like Food, Transportation, Entertainment, etc. You can assign each transaction to the most appropriate category for better spending analysis.

What kind of reports can I generate?

The dashboard provides various reports including monthly spending summaries, category-wise breakdowns, budget vs. actual spending comparisons, and cash flow analysis.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties.

How do I set up budget alerts?

Budget alerts are automatically configured based on your spending patterns. You'll receive notifications on the dashboard when you're approaching or exceeding your budget limits.

Can I export my transaction data?

Yes! The Transactions page includes export buttons that allow you to download your data in CSV or PDF format for external analysis or record-keeping.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

Currently, password reset functionality is not implemented. Please contact support for assistance with password recovery.

Still have questions?

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, feel free to contact us at support@smartexpensestracker.com
